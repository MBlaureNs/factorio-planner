Usage
Select number of each final product desired per minute on the left.
Press 'Cook' to compute assemblers and raw materials required.
Decimal values are allowed. Negatives are not.

Notes
Products produced at chemical plants are currently not working: They are considered raw materials instead.
Notably, sulfur, plastic bars, and all crude oil products are incorrectly considered raw materials.

Images are broken for all fluid materials, including sulfuric acid, lubricant, and water.

Occasionally the program blows up and spits out undefined or hangs due to not receiving a timely response from the database.
Refresh the page to fix the problem, maybe.

Program does not account for solid fuel required to fuel non-electric furnaces under raw materials.
Program does not account for energy required to power inserters to feed materials to assemblers.
Program assumes that all assemblers are assembling machine 2, all furnaces are stone furnaces, and no modules used.

Probably should add tooltips for better usability.
